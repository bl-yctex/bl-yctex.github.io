Bico fiber

Since 1983 ,we has researched in development, and manufacture of bicomponent fiber advanced. Bicomponent  fiber are "co-extruded" with two different polymers in the cross section.  This allows the fiber to use the properties of both materials, and vastly expands the array of possible fiber performance characteristics.  Bicomponent fibers are available in both staple and filament forms.
Mainly type of sheath/core is composed of self bonding fibers, consisting of a low melting temperature sheath surrounding a higher melting temperature core. Common sheath/core combinations in such applications include PE/PP, PE/PET, CO-PET/PET, and PA/PET.  These products are used in 100% form as well as in blends with homopolymer fiber.  Staple fiber applications of these products in non-woven fabrics is by far today’s largest commercial use of bicomponent fibers.
